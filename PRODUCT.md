# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Data stewards, procurement analysts, engineering mentors, and reviewers use DataLens on a desktop during focused data-quality review.
Their primary job is to validate a paired vendor and transaction dataset, understand ranked findings, record review decisions, and inspect whether feedback improves ranking quality.

## Product Purpose

DataLens demonstrates a trustworthy human-in-the-loop procurement data-quality workflow from upload through validation, scoring, review feedback, retraining, and promotion evidence.
Success means a reviewer can understand why a record was flagged, distinguish deterministic severity from model confidence, and verify that no model is activated without passing explicit gates.

## Brand Personality

Calm, rigorous, and candid.
The interface should feel like a well-made analytical instrument rather than a sales dashboard.

## Anti-references

The product should not resemble a fraud-detection console, a neon security operations center, or a generic card-heavy SaaS dashboard.
It should not overstate model confidence, hide failed gates, or use decorative visuals that compete with evidence.

## Design Principles

- Make the next review action obvious.
- Keep evidence and limitations close to every model claim.
- Separate business severity, review priority, and model confidence.
- Prefer familiar analytical controls over invented interactions.
- Show failed gates as clearly as passed gates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard operation.
Do not rely on color alone for severity or promotion status.
Keep motion minimal and respect reduced-motion preferences.
